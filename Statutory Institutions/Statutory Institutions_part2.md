## <a id="q12"></a>Q12. Discuss mechanism for settling inter-state disputes. (2020, 10 Marks)

### ğŸ¯ Core Theme
Inter-state dispute resolution in India employs multifaceted constitutional and legal mechanisms including Article 131 granting Supreme Court exclusive original jurisdiction for direct adjudication (Cauvery dispute example), Inter-State Council under Article 263 for collaborative dialogue, River Water Disputes Act 1956 creating specialized tribunals with binding verdicts (Ravi-Beas case), and Zonal Councils established by States Reorganisation Act 1956 for regional consultation, all underpinned by federal harmony principles requiring political will and cooperative federalism spirit for effective resolution.
